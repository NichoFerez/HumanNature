Human Nature

Who did you used to be, and why did you do away with them?

How do you want people to see you and remember you?

Do you feel like you recall memories at the worst times, or when you're least expecting it?

Do you shut those bad memories down?

Do you feel like you're burdened with the task of covering your tracks?

Do you force yourself to stop talking to the people that were close to you during times you want to forget?

If so, have you brought anyone with you? What is your relationship to them? Do they threaten your new identity?
